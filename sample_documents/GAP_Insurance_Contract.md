## Coverage Details

This Guaranteed Asset Protection (GAP) insurance policy provides coverage for the difference between:
1. The actual cash value of the insured vehicle at the time of total loss
2. The outstanding loan balance owed to the lienholder

**Coverage Amount:** Up to $50,000  
**Term:** 60 months from effective date  
**Premium:** $895.00 (paid in full)

## Covered Events

This policy covers total loss due to:
- Collision
- Comprehensive loss (theft, fire, vandalism, natural disasters)
- Unrecovered stolen vehicle (after 30 days)

## Exclusions

Coverage does not apply to:
- Wear and tear or mechanical breakdown
- Loss due to fraud or intentional acts
- Extended warranties or service contracts
- Negative equity from prior vehicle trade-in
- Late payment penalties or fees

## Claims Process

In the event of a total loss:

1. Contact your primary auto insurance company immediately
2. Obtain the settlement letter from your insurance company
3. Contact GAP Insurance Claims Department within 30 days
4. Submit required documentation:
   - This GAP policy
   - Primary insurance settlement letter
   - Current payoff statement from lienholder
   - Proof of loss from insurance company
   - Odometer statement at time of loss
   - Police report (if applicable)
