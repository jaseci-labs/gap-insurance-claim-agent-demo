## IMPORTANT NOTICES

### Total Loss Claims

If this payoff is for a total loss insurance claim:

- The insurance settlement check must be endorsed by all parties listed on the title
- We will release the vehicle title to the insurance company or salvage buyer upon receipt of full payment
- Title will be released within 10 business days of cleared funds
- If there is a remaining balance after insurance payment, you are responsible for paying the difference unless covered by GAP insurance

### GAP Insurance

If you have Guaranteed Asset Protection (GAP) insurance:

- Contact your GAP insurance provider immediately with this payoff statement
- Provide them with the insurance settlement letter
- GAP insurance typically covers the difference between the insurance settlement and your loan balance
- Keep copies of all correspondence

### After Payoff

Once your loan is paid in full:

- You will receive a lien release letter within 15 business days
- The title will be released to you or forwarded to insurance/salvage company as directed
- Any overpayment will be refunded within 30 days
- Account will be reported as "Paid in Full" to credit bureaus
